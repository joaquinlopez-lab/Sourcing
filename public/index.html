Investment Thesis
We back technical founders building vertical AI applications with clear enterprise distribution. We prioritize founders with deep domain expertise — ex-FAANG, ex-operator, or industry insider — who have an unfair advantage in their market. We believe the next wave of AI value will be captured by vertical-specific applications, not horizontal platforms.
Target Sectors
Vertical AI Fintech Healthcare Tech Cybersecurity SaaS Climate Tech EdTech PropTech
Ideal Founder Profile
✦ Ex-FAANG or deep domain operator — built something before or worked in the belly of the beast
✦ NYC-based or NY-connected — active in the ecosystem
✦ Technical or technical co-founder — can ship fast, owns the product
✦ Pre-seed to Seed — early enough that we can add real value
✦ Stealth or low-profile — we want to find them before everyone else does
Search Criteria
⬡ Raising or recently raised $500K – $10M
⬡ NYC metro area — Manhattan, Brooklyn, or remote NYC-affiliated
⬡ Company age < 3 years
⬡ Enterprise or prosumer distribution — not pure consumer
⬡ AI-native or heavily AI-augmented product
